## Page Purpose
- Home: summarize offer and push primary conversion.
- About: trust and method explanation.
- Services hub: route users to specific service intent.
- Service pages: intent-specific relevance + estimate CTA.
- Service areas: geography confidence and qualification.
- Contact: primary full-detail conversion endpoint.

## Primary CTA Per Page
- Home: `Get Free Estimate`
- Service pages: `Request Estimate`
- Contact: `Submit Estimate Request`
- Footer/global: `Call (512) 361-4887`

## Internal Linking Model
- Home -> services pages and contact.
- Service pages -> contact and related services.
- Header/footer persistent links -> service areas and contact.
- Legacy service routes -> mapped to new intent pages or retained parity paths.
